Our Story

Who We Are

At Aarohi Enterprises, we recognized the growing demand for high-quality laboratory and quality control products — and made it our mission to meet it. We supply a comprehensive range of lab essentials including chemicals, glassware, instruments, filter papers, and much more.

Driven by a quality-first strategy and a commitment to quick delivery, we have built a reputation for supplying a wide range of premium laboratory products. Our entire product line is stocked in our own warehouse in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, enabling fast and reliable fulfilment.

We deal exclusively in ISI-marked glassware for quality control labs, and we take pride in offering our clients across the chemical and allied industries the safest, least toxic products available. Behind every order is a dedicated procurement team that sources only from trusted, reliable companies.
